About
M-Hexad Solution

M-Hexad Solution is a professional consultancy company specializing in MM2H application services, relocation assistance, visa advisory, and settlement support for foreigners planning to make Malaysia their second home.

We understand that relocating to another country is an important life decision. Our dedicated consultants work closely with clients to ensure a smooth, transparent, and stress-free process from consultation to approval and relocation.

With offices in Malaysia and Bangladesh, we serve clients globally and provide personalized consultation tailored to each client's needs and goals.

Our Journey

Our Growth Story

2016
Company Founded

M-Hexad Solution was established with a vision to simplify the MM2H process for international clients seeking to relocate to Malaysia.

2018
Malaysia Office Established

Opened our flagship office in Seremban, Negeri Sembilan, Malaysia — providing on-ground support for clients and direct liaison with Malaysian authorities.

Founder & Managing Director

Mahendran Maruthai

With nearly two decades of experience in international business development, strategic partnerships and client advisory services, Mahendran Maruthai has built a distinguished career connecting people, businesses and opportunities across Malaysia and the global marketplace.

Over the years, he has worked extensively with clients, institutions and organisations throughout Asia, Africa, the Middle East and Europe, developing a deep understanding of the needs of international families, expatriates, investors and professionals seeking opportunities in Malaysia.

Mahendran is passionate about showcasing Malaysia as one of Asia's most attractive destinations for long-term residency, retirement, investment, healthcare, education and quality of life. Under his leadership, M-Hexad Solution continues to build strong relationships with international partners and deliver trusted advisory services to clients from around the world who aspire to call Malaysia their second home.
