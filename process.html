How It Works

Your MM2H Journey in
5 Expert-Guided Steps

We have refined our process over years of experience to make your MM2H journey as smooth and stress-free as possible. Every step is managed by our expert team.

01
Starting Point

Initial Consultation

We begin with a comprehensive one-on-one consultation — in person, by video call, or phone — where our expert consultants evaluate your profile, financial standing, and goals. We discuss the most suitable MM2H category and pathway for your specific situation, and provide a clear overview of requirements, timelines, and expected outcomes.

Profile Assessment
Programme Selection
Requirement Briefing
02
Preparation Phase

Documentation Preparation

Our consultants provide you with a detailed, personalized checklist of all required supporting documents. We assist you in preparing, verifying, and organizing each document — including financial statements, medical certificates, insurance documents, personal declarations, and family documentation — ensuring everything meets the official requirements.

Document Checklist
Document Verification
Translation & Notarization
03
Submission Phase

Application Submission

We coordinate the complete application submission process, liaising directly with the Malaysian Ministry of Tourism, Arts and Culture (MOTAC) and other relevant authorities on your behalf. Our team ensures all forms are accurately completed and submitted within the prescribed guidelines, minimizing delays and maximizing approval prospects.

Official Submission
Authority Liaison
Progress Updates
04
Approval Phase

Approval & Visa Processing

Once conditional approval is received from the Malaysian Government, we guide you through the next critical steps — including fixed deposit placement, medical examination in Malaysia, and final visa endorsement at the Immigration Department. We accompany you through every requirement to ensure successful completion.

Conditional Approval
Fixed Deposit Setup
Visa Endorsement
05
Arrival & Beyond

Relocation & Settlement

With your MM2H visa in hand, we help you settle comfortably in Malaysia. Our relocation support includes assistance with accommodation, school enrollment for children, banking setup, utility connections, and connecting you with trusted local service providers. Our relationship with you doesn't end at approval — it's just the beginning.

Accommodation Search
School Enrollment
Banking & Settlement
