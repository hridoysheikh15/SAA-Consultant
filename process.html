How It Works

Your MM2H Journey in
8 Expert-Guided Steps

We have refined our process over years of practice to make every MM2H application as smooth and stress-free as possible. Each of the 8 stages is fully managed by our dedicated team.

01
Starting point

Free Consultation

Begin with a no-obligation consultation — in person, via video call, or by phone. Our MM2H specialists review your nationality, financial profile, family situation and goals to provide honest and accurate initial guidance at no cost.

No cost, no obligation
In-person or online
Bilingual support available
02
Assessment

Eligibility Assessment

We conduct a thorough eligibility check against the current MM2H requirements. We determine which tier — Silver, Gold or Platinum — is the best fit for your age, income and asset profile, and provide a clear, personalised action plan with defined timelines and estimated costs.

Tier selection
Gap analysis
Personalised roadmap
03
Preparation phase

Document Preparation

We provide a detailed, personalised document checklist and guide you through the collection and preparation of all required materials — bank statements, financial certificates, medical examination, insurance documents, marriage certificates, birth certificates and more. We review and verify every document before submission.

Personalised checklist
Document verification
Translation & notarisation
04
Official submission

Submission to Authorities

We coordinate and manage the complete official application submission directly to the Malaysian Ministry of Tourism, Arts and Culture (MOTAC). Our team handles all liaison with authorities, tracks your application status and keeps you updated throughout the review period.

Submitted to MOTAC
Authority liaison
Status tracking
05
Conditional approval

Approval Letter

Upon successful review, MOTAC issues a conditional approval letter. We receive and review the letter on your behalf, explain the conditions clearly, and prepare you for the next steps to be completed within Malaysia. This is a major milestone on your MM2H journey.

MOTAC approval
Conditions explained
Next-step planning
06
Financial commitment

Fixed Deposit Setup

We accompany or guide you through opening a Malaysian bank account and placing the required fixed deposit in a Government-approved commercial bank. We help you select the most suitable bank, complete the process efficiently and obtain the required confirmation letter for visa endorsement.

Bank account opening
FD placement
Confirmation letter
07
Final step in Malaysia

Visa Endorsement

We schedule and accompany you (or your authorised representative) to the Immigration Department of Malaysia for the final visa endorsement. The MM2H social visit pass is stamped into your passport, making your long-term residency in Malaysia official. Your dependants' passes are endorsed at the same time.

Immigration appointment
Visa stamped
Dependants endorsed
08
Welcome to Malaysia

Relocation to Malaysia

With your MM2H pass in hand, we support your full transition into Malaysian life. Our relocation team assists with accommodation search, international school enrolment for children, utility setup, transport arrangements, and connecting you with trusted local service providers. Our relationship does not end at approval — it is just the beginning.

Accommodation
School enrolment
Settlement support
