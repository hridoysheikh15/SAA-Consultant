About the MM2H Programme

MM2H (Malaysia My Second Home) is a long-term Malaysian residency programme for eligible foreign nationals, introduced by the Malaysian Government. It grants a renewable multiple-entry social visit pass allowing holders to live in Malaysia on a long-term basis. The programme is designed to attract retirees, investors, families, and international professionals who wish to make Malaysia their second home.

No, MM2H is not permanent residency. It is a renewable long-term social visit pass programme, typically granted for 5 years and renewable subject to continued compliance with the programme's conditions. It does not automatically lead to permanent residency or Malaysian citizenship.

The MM2H visa is typically issued for a period of 5 years and is renewable. Holders must continue to meet the programme's ongoing conditions — including maintaining the required fixed deposit and insurance — to successfully renew their pass.

The MM2H programme is managed by the Malaysian Ministry of Tourism, Arts and Culture (MOTAC), which handles all applications and programme administration. The Immigration Department of Malaysia handles visa endorsements and processing.

Eligibility & Requirements

The MM2H Programme is open to all foreign nationals regardless of nationality, provided they meet the financial and documentation requirements set by the Malaysian Government. This includes retirees, investors, business owners, families, and digital professionals who can demonstrate the required financial capacity and meet the health and documentation criteria.

Financial requirements vary by category. The Silver tier (below 35) requires RM 500,000 in liquid assets and a RM 150,000 fixed deposit. The Gold tier (35–49) requires RM 750,000 in liquid assets and a RM 250,000 fixed deposit. The Platinum tier (50+) requires RM 1,000,000 in liquid assets and a RM 150,000 fixed deposit. All categories require proof of offshore monthly income of at least RM 40,000.

There is no minimum age requirement for main applicants. However, the programme's three categories — Silver, Gold, and Platinum — are differentiated by age brackets (below 35, 35–49, and 50+) with different financial requirements for each. Dependent children may be included up to age 34.

Family & Dependants

Yes. Eligible dependants including your spouse and children may be included in your MM2H application. Children may be included up to age 34, and the programme also allows inclusion of one domestic helper. All dependants must meet the relevant health and documentation requirements.

Yes. Dependent children of MM2H holders can attend schools in Malaysia, including the country's extensive network of international schools. Malaysia hosts over 130 international schools following British, American, Australian, and IB curricula.

Property & Lifestyle

Yes, MM2H holders are permitted to purchase property in Malaysia, subject to state-specific property regulations and the minimum purchase price requirements set for foreign buyers (typically RM 1 million or above, varying by state and property type). Our property consultation team can guide you through the best options for your needs.

Under the current MM2H framework, the Platinum tier allows visa holders to engage in limited part-time employment (up to 20 hours per week) with a minimum salary of RM 10,000 per month, subject to specific employer and sector approvals. The Silver and Gold tiers do not permit employment. Full-time employment requires a separate work permit.

Application Process

Processing duration varies depending on documentation completeness, application volume at MOTAC, and approval timelines. Generally, applicants can expect the process from initial submission to conditional approval to take between 3 to 6 months. Our team works to minimize delays by ensuring all documentation is accurate and complete from the outset.

The initial application preparation can be done from your home country. However, once conditional approval is received, applicants are typically required to visit Malaysia for the medical examination, fixed deposit placement, and final visa endorsement at the Immigration Department. Our team provides full support during your visit.
