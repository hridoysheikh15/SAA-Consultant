What is the MM2H Programme?

The Malaysia My Second Home (MM2H) Programme is a long-term residency initiative launched by the Malaysian Government to attract eligible foreign nationals to live in Malaysia on a renewable multiple-entry social visit pass. Since its relaunch in 2021, the programme has been restructured into three distinct tiers designed to accommodate different financial profiles and life stages.

For families, retirees, investors, and digital entrepreneurs seeking an affordable yet high-quality Asian lifestyle, the MM2H programme remains one of the most competitive long-term residency options in the region. Malaysia's strategic location, English-speaking environment, multicultural society, and modern infrastructure make it an extraordinarily attractive destination.

"The MM2H Programme is not just a visa — it's an invitation to experience one of Asia's most vibrant and diverse nations as your genuine second home."

The Three MM2H Categories

The updated MM2H programme introduced three categories — Silver, Gold, and Platinum — each with distinct financial requirements designed to cater to applicants at different life stages and wealth levels.

Silver Category (Below 35 Years)

The Silver tier targets younger applicants below the age of 35. Applicants are required to demonstrate offshore income of at least RM 40,000 per month, maintain a fixed deposit of RM 150,000, and show liquid assets of RM 500,000. This tier is particularly attractive for digital entrepreneurs and younger international professionals seeking a Southeast Asian base.

Gold Category (35–49 Years)

The Gold tier serves the mid-career professional and family relocation segment. Requirements include offshore monthly income of RM 40,000, a fixed deposit of RM 250,000, and liquid assets of RM 750,000. This is the most popular category for international families relocating for education and lifestyle reasons.

Platinum Category (50+ Years)

The Platinum tier caters to retirees and high-net-worth individuals aged 50 and above. It requires offshore monthly income of RM 40,000, a fixed deposit of RM 150,000, and liquid assets of RM 1,000,000. The Platinum tier also offers greater flexibility in terms of property purchase and dependents.

Key Benefits of MM2H

MM2H visa holders enjoy a range of significant benefits that make long-term residence in Malaysia highly rewarding. These include a renewable 5-year multiple-entry social visit pass, the ability to include spouse and dependent children, access to Malaysia's excellent healthcare and international school system, and the opportunity to purchase property subject to state regulations.

Additionally, MM2H holders can bring one domestic helper into Malaysia, and the Platinum tier allows visa holders to engage in limited part-time employment (maximum 20 hours per week) at an approved monthly salary of not less than RM 10,000.
