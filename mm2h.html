Program Overview

What is
MM2H?

The Malaysia My Second Home (MM2H) Programme is a long-term residency programme introduced by the Malaysian Government to allow eligible foreign nationals to stay in Malaysia with a renewable multiple-entry social visit pass.

Malaysia offers a high quality of life, affordable living costs, excellent healthcare, international schools, and a peaceful multicultural environment that makes it attractive for retirees, families, investors, and digital entrepreneurs.
