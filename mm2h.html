Programme overview

What is
MM2H?

The Malaysia My Second Home (MM2H) Programme is a long-term residency initiative by the Malaysian Government that allows eligible foreign nationals to live in Malaysia on a renewable multiple-entry social visit pass.

Restructured in 2021 and refined further, MM2H now operates under three distinct tiers — Silver, Gold and Platinum — each designed to accommodate different age groups and financial profiles.

Fixed deposit

Fixed Deposit
Requirements

All MM2H applicants are required to place a fixed deposit in an approved Malaysian bank account as part of the approval conditions. This must be completed within a specified period after receiving conditional approval.

Tier FD Amount Timing
Silver RM 150,000 Within 6 months of approval
Gold RM 250,000 Within 6 months of approval
Platinum RM 150,000 Within 6 months of approval
  • Must be placed in an approved Malaysian commercial bank
  • Interest earned can be used for living expenses
  • Up to 50% withdrawable after year 1 for approved purposes (property, vehicle, education, medical)
  • Full amount must be maintained throughout visa validity
Offshore income

Offshore Income
Requirements

All three MM2H tiers require applicants to demonstrate a minimum monthly offshore income. This income must be sourced from outside Malaysia and documented through official bank statements or income certificates.

Tier Monthly Income Annual Equivalent
Silver RM 40,000 RM 480,000+
Gold RM 40,000 RM 480,000+
Platinum RM 40,000 RM 480,000+
  • Income must originate from outside Malaysia
  • Accepted sources: salary, pension, dividends, rental income, business income
  • Must be supported by 3–6 months of bank statements
  • Retirees may use pension statements as proof
